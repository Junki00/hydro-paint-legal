Privacy Policy for Hydro Paint

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Hydro Paint respects your privacy. This app is designed to help users track hydration progress and display visual artwork experiences on device.

Information We Collect

Hydro Paint does not collect personal information, does not create user accounts, and does not send personal data to external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

Hydro Paint stores app data locally on your device in order to support core functionality, such as hydration progress, selected artwork, and widget display state.

  • Daily hydration records
  • Your selected painting or visual preferences
  • Widget-related local shared data used between the app and its widget extension

Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Hydro Paint does not use third-party analytics, does not track users across apps or websites, and does not serve targeted advertising.

Third-Party Services

Hydro Paint does not rely on third-party services for user analytics, advertising, or cloud-based personal data processing as part of the current app experience.

Children's Privacy

Hydro Paint does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

Because your data is stored locally on your own device, you may remove app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
